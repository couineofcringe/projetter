Who am I ? What do I do ?

After studying Bioengineering, I realised that I wanted to do something a little more creative while retaining some technical skills.
The UX UI domain striked me as the perfect balance between my scientific expertise and creativity, combining digital innovation and user-centered design.
My previous experience has enabled me to sharpen my critical, analytical, and methodological thinking in terms of problem resolutions. I enjoy a lot putting this at use when approching a UX case. It almost feels like solving a puzzle. However, it's the creative dimension through the UI Design that really excites me.
As someone who is sensitive to issues of accessibility and inclusiveness, I look forward to the opportunity of being involved in projects that promote them. As I'm still a novice, I'm very keen to learn. I'm meticulous and deeply commited to my learning.
